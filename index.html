Strawberry Pound Cake

Strawberry pound cake is a really good dessert to try, especially if your favorite fruit is strawberries. It is also really fun and easy to make. By following these easy instructions, you can make a delicious cake to share with your family and friends!!

Photo credits to Immaculate bites

Ingredients:

  • Strawberries
  • Unsalted butter
  • Sugar
  • Eggs
  • All purpose flour
  • Baking soda and Baking powder
  • Vanilla Extract
  • Strawberry Extract
  • Red food coloring
  • Lemon zest
  • Sour cream
  • Powedered Sugar and Strawberry Puree (optional Strawberry Glaze)

Recipe:

  1. Preheat the oven to 325 degree and grease a pan with non-stick baking spray
  2. Mix butter and sugar at a high speed in mixer or hand beater for 5-7 minutes until it is fluffy and starts looking white
  3. Add the eggs one at a time and mix while adding it
  4. Mix the flour, baking powder,baking soda, and add a little salt and a little sour cream for flavor into the batter
  5. Add both strawberry and vanilla extract and mix the batter well or until it is thorougly combined
  6. To make the strawberry puree, add strawberries and 2-3 teaspoons of red food coloring into the batter and mix
  7. Once completed, pour batter into greased pan and bake at 300 degree Farenheit
  8. To make strawberry glaze,add the remaining strawberries, lemon zest, strawberry extract, powdered sugar and mix until smooth
  9. Once cake is finished, pour the glaze over it and enjoy
